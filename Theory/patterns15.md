Here are **15 core algorithm patterns** that cover the majority of coding interview / DSA problems.

1. **Prefix Sum**
2. **Two Pointers**
3. **Sliding Window**
4. **Fast & Slow Pointers (Tortoise–Hare)**
5. **Binary Search**
6. **Monotonic Stack**
7. **Monotonic Queue / Deque**
8. **Heap / Priority Queue**
9. **Greedy (Local Optimal Choice)**
10. **Backtracking**
11. **Dynamic Programming (DP)**
12. **Graph Traversal (DFS / BFS)**
13. **Topological Sort**
14. **Union Find (Disjoint Set)**
15. **Trie (Prefix Tree)**

These patterns appear constantly on platforms like **LeetCode**, **HackerRank**, and **Codeforces**.

If you learn to **recognize these patterns**, most problems become much easier to solve.
